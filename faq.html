Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer flavored coconut water options?

Absolutely! At Coconut Oasis, we understand the desire for variety. That's why we offer an assortment of flavored coconut water options for you to enjoy, ranging from tropical blends to subtle infusions.

Can I order online and pick up my coconut water?

Yes, at Coconut Oasis, we offer the convenience of online ordering with pick-up options. You can place your order through our website and collect your refreshing coconut water at your convenience.

Are your coconut waters available in different sizes?

Certainly! We offer a range of sizes for our coconut water to suit your needs, whether you prefer a small bottle for on-the-go or a larger one to share with friends.

Are your coconut waters sweetened by default?

No, at Coconut Oasis, our coconut waters are naturally sweetened by the coconut itself. We aim to provide a pure and refreshing experience without any added sugars or sweeteners.
